SK Landscaping

Landscape design, installation, and repair

Quality work you can trust

Our love for plants and the outdoors inspires us to put careful attention and hard work into every one of our projects. With 25 years in landscaping experience, we can offer your expert guidance on how to design, repair, or modify any outdoor space. Whether you want to install stonework, fix a drainage issue, control weeds, repair or install an irrigation system, prune trees and shrubs, or just make your yard more eco-friendly, we'd be happy to help you find a solution.

Detailed workmanship

We bring an attention to detail and an artistic eye to every project we tackle, especially lasting features like walls, walkways, and ponds. Details like the curve of a path, the use of natural rocks, or a change in grading can turn even a small yard into a natural-looking garden.

Drought-conscious designs

From the plants you grow to the slope of your land, every aspect of your landscape affects your water use. Through run-off control, improved grading, the use of native plants, and drip irrigation installation, you can update your yard to conserve natural resources and your water bill.
